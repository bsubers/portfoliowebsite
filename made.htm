Made Magazine

Where: Cabrini College

When: Spring 2012

“Made – American Living, Lifestyle and Goods”, is a magazine that focuses on towns, companies, and events in the United States of America. The masthead design is an homage to industrial stamps that were common place in The USA years ago. It should bring back a sense of pride in the craftsmanship that can be found in our wonderful country. This issue features the town of Wayne, PA as its main story, a long with other featured stories, and department contents. Some of the other articles include an interview with Jack White, a visit to the Icon Derelicts Garage, and Cycling 101.
