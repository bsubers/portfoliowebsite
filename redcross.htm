Red Cross Ad Campaign

Course: Graphic Design IV

Where: Cabrini College

When: Spring 2013

View live website

For my undergraduate capstone project, I was tasked with picking a non profit to creative an ad campaign for, and then build a responsive website to go along with it. Since donating blood is something I do as often as possible, I settled on the Red Cross.

My concept steamed from the idea that people don’t really understand how big of an impact their donation can make. I used a play on words with the different blood types.

The website was my first time learning responsive web design, so it was a bit of a challenge. I am proud of what I was able to do with the knowledge I had at the time. Since then, my skills have increased exponentially.
