Helicopter Interface

Course: Interactive Design Class

Where: Cabrini College

When: August 2012 - December 2012

This was a project in multimedia design class, completed in fall 2012. The task was to design an informative interface using Adobe Flash. I choose to make mine on Helicopters. The kiosk shows the history, air dynamics, cockpit, models, and videos for helicopters. It could be used in an aviation museum, coupled with a display of parts and gear.

Wireframes

I sketched and created lo fidelity wireframes in illustrator to get ideas down. I then gathered art and crafted the final interface.
