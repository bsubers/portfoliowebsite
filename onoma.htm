Ónoma Digital

What: Branding

When: May 2014

I was contracted as a freelance designer to create an identity for a startup digital marketing company. 

In the Greek language, Onoma translates to “the manifestation, or revelation of someone’s character”. I worked to capture the definition by using the “O” and “D” to create a character for the brand. The mark on the top of this character is not only significant to the language of origin, but also signifies ideation.
