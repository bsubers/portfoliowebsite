Haiti

When: Fall 2011

Where: Cabrini College

Course: Color Theory

This poster was designed to inform the public of the aide required in Haiti after the tragic earthquake that struck in 2010, affecting nearly 3.5 million people. 

I envisioned a poster that would blend both tragedy and hope together in one design. The buildings were illustrated using very basic shapes, then sliced and positioned askew in order to appear damaged. The colors I chose to utilize were pulled from an image of vibrantly painted Haitian buildings. Once the poster catches the attention of the viewer, a very straight-forward call to action “Help Rebuild Haiti” can be seen at the bottom of the composition. This leads into a description of the effects this tragedy has had on Haiti, and how everyone can support the cause.
